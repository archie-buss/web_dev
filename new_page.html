Color Theory Practice

Color theory is the study of how colors interact and the visual impacts they create. Practicing with color combinations helps you build palettes that communicate mood and meaning.

Below is a simple color wheel image and a short list of basic color relationships. Hover over the faint square hotspot (top-left of the wheel) to reveal a bonus illustrative image.

  • Complementary: colors opposite on the wheel
  • Analogous: colors next to each other on the wheel
  • Triadic: three evenly spaced colors
