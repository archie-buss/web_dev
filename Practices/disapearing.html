All About Penguins

Penguins are fascinating birds that live primarily in the Southern Hemisphere. Even though they cannot fly, they are excellent swimmers and use their wings like flippers.

There are many species of penguins, each adapted to unique environments. Penguins are known for their tuxedo-like feathers, social behavior, and adorable waddle.
